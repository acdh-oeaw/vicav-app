How to use the VICAV Web-App?

VICAV Web-App is a map based web application and following the six basic steps listed below you can easily start exploring our project, finding interesting resources for your research and even contribute to improve our data:
Find dictionaries and resources from the map using map functions.
Explore different aspects of our project using the top navigation menu.
Use panels to navigate through sources and make comparisons.
Custom search queries on panels enable you to refine your results.
Propose your contributions to help grow our research.
Your contributions will be reviewed and published, crediting your name.
