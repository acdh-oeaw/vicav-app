    What can I do with the VICAV platform?

    Dive into the world of Arabic dialects either through the map or through the menu
    Study with bibliographies, linguistic profiles, sample texts, dictionaries, ...
    Compare ling. phenomena in collapsible panels
    Learn about digital humanities, text technology and digital standards
    Take part in a cooperative project by contributing resources. We always need help!
